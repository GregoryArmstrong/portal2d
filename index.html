Portal 2D

Controls

  • A: Shoot Portal Left
  • W: Shoot Portal Up
  • D: Shoot Portal Right
  • X: Shoot Portal Down
  • Shift: Switch Portal Color
  • Left Arrow: Move Left
  • Up Arrow: Jump
  • Right Arrow: Move Right

Game Tips

  • When a portal-projectile strikes a wall, a portal is opened.

  • If both a blue and orange portal exist, the player teleports from one to the other upon entering either portal.

  • Falling in lava will result in your death.

  • Upon reaching the door, you move on to the next laboratory room.
